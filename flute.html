Saesha'z Zone!!! ------Saesha on Flute!!!

This page summarizes my experience as teacher and performer on flute!

I earned my bachelor's degree in flute performance from Boise State University. During this time, I also performed professionally in the Boise metropolitan area. While at University of Washington for my master's degree in performance, I participated in the vibrant musical culture of Seattle, WA. Although my official studies ended after my time at West Virginia University, I continued performing and still enjoy doing so today.

Currently, I am available to perform or teach. Just send me an email below!
